Keep Doing

Step 1:

All schedules are listed in these three tabs. In the TODO tab, schedules are given different colours to denote the serious level, the redder it is, the closer the due date is.

Step 2:

You can add a schedule by clicking the plus button at the right-bottom of the screen. On the Add Schedule page, all inputs are required, and also you can specify when the app reminds you.

Step 3:

You can update a schedule by clicking the list item. All information can be modified

Step 4:

If you done or want to dismiss the schedule, you can click "DONE" or "DISMISS" button. this schedule will be putted into DONE tab or deleted

Step 5:

If this schedule is overdue, it will be putted into OVERDUE Tab.

Step 6:

You can choose your favorite serious colors by clicking the menu item "Change Color"
